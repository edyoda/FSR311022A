New Tags in HTML 5 : 

main, section, header, footer, article, aside, 


<pre> = Preformatted text => Takes the text inside as it is and print it on the browser i.e same space and line breaks and alignmnets that are given to it.

<details> and <summary> => Used to show the details which are hidden by default and when you click on summary, the detail appears 

<figure> and <figcaption> => Used to create group of image and its caption
